FAQ

How do I set up a VPN connection?

VPN-RHD supports sing-box based configurations. Open the Profiles tab, tap the add button, and import your server configuration via subscription link or by pasting a manual config. Then toggle the VPN switch on the Home screen to connect.

What video sites are supported?

The download feature is powered by yt-dlp, which supports thousands of websites. Paste a video URL in the Download tab and choose MP4 (video) or MP3 (audio) format.

Where are downloaded files saved?

Files are saved in the App's local storage. Access them via the folder icon in the Download tab. From there you can play videos, share files to other apps, or save MP4 videos to your Photos library.

Can I batch download or delete files?

Yes. Tap "Select" in the Downloaded Files screen to enter batch mode. Select multiple files to batch share, save to Photos, or delete. You can also long-press any file for quick actions or swipe left to delete.

Does the VPN keep logs?

VPN-RHD itself does not log anything. It is a client-side app that connects to servers you configure. Any logging depends on the VPN server you choose to connect to.
