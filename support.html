Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up a VPN connection?

VPN-RHD supports sing-box based configurations. Import your server configuration (subscription link or manual config) in the Profiles tab, then toggle the VPN switch on the Home screen.

What video sites are supported for downloads?

The download feature is powered by yt-dlp, which supports thousands of sites. Simply paste a video URL and choose your preferred format (MP4 video or MP3 audio).

Where are downloaded files saved?

Files are saved in the App's Documents folder. You can access them via the "Downloaded Files" list (tap the folder icon in the Download tab), share them, or save MP4 videos to your Photos library.

Why can't I save audio files to Photos?

iOS Photos only supports video formats. MP3 and other audio files can be shared to other apps via the Share button.

Does the VPN keep logs?

No. VPN-RHD is a client-side app only. It does not operate any VPN servers and does not log your traffic. Logging depends entirely on the VPN server you connect to.
