Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Overview

VPN-RHD ("the App") is a VPN client and media download tool for iOS. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use the App.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history or network traffic
  • VPN connection logs or timestamps
  • Download history or video URLs
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers beyond what iOS provides for essential app functionality

3. VPN Configuration

All VPN server configurations and credentials are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your server information or connection details. The App acts solely as a client for the VPN protocols you configure.

4. Media Downloads

When you use the download feature:

  • Video URLs you enter are sent directly to yt-dlp (an open-source download engine) running locally on your device.
  • Downloaded files are saved to the App's sandboxed local storage.
  • We do not intercept, log, or store any URLs or downloaded content.
  • Photo Library access occurs only when you explicitly choose to save a file. The App never accesses your photos otherwise.

5. Embedded Third-Party Components

The App embeds the following open-source components. All run entirely on-device with no external communication:

  • sing-box (via Libbox framework) — VPN tunnel core
  • yt-dlp — video and audio download engine
  • FFmpeg — media encoding and transcoding
  • Python 3.14 — embedded scripting runtime

6. Network Access

The App uses network access solely for: (a) establishing VPN tunnels to servers you configure, and (b) downloading media from URLs you provide. There is no background data collection, telemetry, or analytics.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
