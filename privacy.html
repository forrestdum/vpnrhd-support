Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Overview

VPN-RHD ("the App") is a VPN client and media download tool for iOS. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use the App.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history or network traffic
  • VPN connection logs or timestamps
  • Download history or video URLs
  • Device identifiers beyond what iOS provides for essential functionality
  • Location data

3. VPN Configuration Data

All VPN server configurations and credentials are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your server information or connection details. The App acts solely as a client for the VPN protocols you configure.

4. Media Downloads

When you use the download feature:

  • Video URLs you enter are sent directly to yt-dlp (an open-source download tool) running locally on your device.
  • Downloaded files are saved to the App's local storage on your device.
  • We do not intercept, log, or store any URLs or downloaded content.
  • Saving to Photos requires your permission; the App accesses the Photo Library only when you explicitly choose to save a file.

5. Third-Party Services

The App embeds the following open-source components internally. These run entirely on-device:

  • yt-dlp - video/audio downloading (no data shared with external servers)
  • FFmpeg - media processing (all processing is local)
  • Python 3.14 runtime - runs embedded, no external communication
  • sing-box / Libbox - VPN core (connects only to the servers you configure)

No data is sent to the developers or any third-party analytics service.

6. Network Access

The App uses network access solely for:

  • Establishing VPN tunnels to servers you configure
  • Downloading media from URLs you provide

There is no background data collection, telemetry, or analytics.

7. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device within the App's sandboxed container, protected by iOS system-level security. VPN credentials are managed by iOS Keychain where applicable.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
